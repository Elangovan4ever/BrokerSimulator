Privacy Policy for Lights Out

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Lights Out is a single-player puzzle game published by SE Labs.

Data collection

Lights Out does not require account creation and does not collect personal data for gameplay.

Ads and analytics

The current release does not serve ads and does not use third-party analytics SDKs.

Permissions

The app does not request sensitive permissions for contacts, location, camera, microphone, health data, or financial data.

Children

Lights Out is intended for general audiences aged 13 and older.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact SE Labs at contact@selabs.ddns.net.
